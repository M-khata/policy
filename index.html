Privacy Policy - M Khata

Effective Date: 01/06/2026

Information We Collect

User profile information (if provided), boat details, fish catch records, fish sales information, expense records, crew salary details, agency and ledger information, device information, app usage information and analytics data.

How We Use Information

Provide app functionality, store records, improve app performance, analyze usage, send notifications, and improve features.

Notifications

M Khata may send business reminders, updates, and app-related notifications. Users can disable notifications in device settings.

Analytics

M Khata uses analytics services to understand app performance, screens visited, app interactions, crash reports, and usage statistics.

Advertising

Future versions may display advertisements. Advertising partners may collect device identifiers and app interaction information.

Data Sharing

M Khata does not sell personal information. Data may be shared only with trusted providers or when required by law.

Data Security

Reasonable security measures are used, but no electronic storage method is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

M Khata is not intended for children under 13 years old.

Changes to Policy

The Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.
