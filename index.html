Privacy Policy for M Khata

Last Updated: June 04, 2026

Welcome to M Khata ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application, M Khata (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Because M Khata is designed to respect your privacy, we collect minimal personal information.

  • User-Provided Information: When using the App, you may optionally enter a Boat Name, Owner Name, Phone Number, and Boat Registration Number. Phone numbers and registration numbers are completely optional.
  • Financial and Ledger Data: Any data you enter regarding fish catches, weights, prices, expenses, crew salaries, and agency ledgers is stored locally on your device only. We do not upload this ledger data to external servers.
  • Storage and Document Access: The App uses the Android System File Picker (Storage Access Framework - SAF) to allow you to export and save PDF reports directly to your device's storage. We do not access, read, or store any other files on your device.

2. Third-Party Services and Data Processing

While we do not store your ledger data on our servers, we use trusted third-party services for monetization, app optimization, and processing payments. These services may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob (Advertisements): We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data (such as your device's advertising ID, IP address, and coarse location data) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads tailored to your interests.
  • Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases): We offer in-app purchases to unlock premium features. All financial transactions are processed securely by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store your credit card details, bank account info, or billing addresses. Google provides us only with anonymous transaction confirmations to activate your purchases.
  • Firebase (Analytics, Notifications, & Remote Config): We use Google Firebase to improve app performance and user experience:
    • Analytics & Crashlytics: To monitor crashes and understand anonymous usage statistics.
    • Cloud Messaging: To send push notifications regarding app updates or features.
    • Remote Config: To dynamically change the behavior and appearance of the App (such as enabling/disabling specific features) without requiring an app update. Firebase Remote Config may use temporary instance IDs to deliver these configurations to your device.

3. Data Storage and Deletion

  • Local Storage: All your ledger entries, business data, and profile details remain on your physical device.
  • Data Deletion: You have full control over your data. You can delete all your data at any time by clearing the App's storage in your device settings or by uninstalling the App. Once uninstalled, all locally stored data is permanently lost and cannot be recovered by us.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is safe for general use and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. However, because we use third-party ad networks and payment processors, we encourage parents to monitor their children's mobile activity.

5. International Jurisdiction

M Khata is operated from Austria but is tailored for users globally, with a specific focus on fishermen in India (including Gujarat). By using the App, you consent to the data processing practices outlined by our third-party partners (Google and Firebase) under applicable privacy standards.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or via Firebase notifications.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: mkhata.team@outlook.com.
